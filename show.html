Vancouver Gem Show

About show:

When: 2-4 November 2019
Where: Canada Place, 999 Canada Pl, Vancouver, British Columbia, Canada
Where: View housing options here
Planning: Hall planning (PDF)

Canada’s Firs Contemporary and Art Jewellery Exibition. Vancouver's thriving economy is part of what makes this city one of the world's most desirable locations for business and leisure. Located in the heart of Vancouver's glittering waterfront, Canada Place is an iconic landmark and venue for world-class events and inspirationally Canadian experiences.

indiGem has produced close to fifty events in Canada and internationally since their inception as Calgary Gem Show back in 2010. Over the past ten years, their Gem and Jewellery Shows have been evolving alongside the rapidly changing digital epoch.

Admittance to indiGem Jewellery show wholesale day is restricted to qualified jewellery retailers only.

Attendees are required to provide valid documentation in order to have access to purchase the wholesale day admittance ticket.

- Government-issued Personal ID - Driver's License or Passport. Identification may not be expired.

- Personal business card or recent pay stub with matching company name.

- A copy of itemized invoice from your company jewellery supplier within the past year.
