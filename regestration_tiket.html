Admittance to indiGem Jewellery show wholesale day is restricted to qualified jewellery retailers only.

Attendees are required to provide valid documentation in order to have access to purchase the wholesale day admittance ticket.

- Government-issued Personal ID - Driver's License or Passport. Identification may not be expired.

- Personal business card or recent pay stub with matching company name.

- A copy of itemized invoice from your company jewellery supplier within the past year.
